Game Overview

Mystic Realms is designed for 2-5 players and one Game Master. The core mechanic revolves around a d20 system, with character skills and abilities adding modifiers to dice rolls. Character creation is flexible, allowing for a wide range of unique heroes. Campaigns can range from single-session adventures to long-running sagas spanning the entire continent of Eldoria.
